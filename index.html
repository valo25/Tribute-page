Here's a time line of Diego Maradona:

  • 1976 - Debut in the first division of Argentina with Argentinos Juniors
  • 1977 - Debut in the national team and his relationship with Claudia Villafañe starts
  • 1981 - Debut in Boca Juniors
  • 1982 - Becomes a Barcelona player
  • 1983 - Won the Copa del Rey
  • 1984 - He got transfered to Napoli
  • 1986 - Won the World Cup and scores two goals against England immortalizing him for all eternity
  • 1987 - Won the Serie A with Napoli and Dalma, his first daughter was born
  • 1989 - Gianina was born
  • 1990 - Runner-up in Italy 1990
  • 1994 - He plays his last game for Argentina with a victory over Nigeria for the World Cup in the United States
  • 1995 - Started his career as a manager at Racing Club
  • 2008 - Is elected coach of the Argentina national team
  • 2020 - Died at the age of 60
